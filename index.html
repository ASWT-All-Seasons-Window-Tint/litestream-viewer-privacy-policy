Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

Thank you for using LiteStream Viewer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

1. Limited Data Collection

LiteStream Viewer is designed to minimize data collection. We do not require personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

However, like most internet-based services, we may automatically collect limited technical data necessary for the app to function, including:

  • IP address

  • Basic usage data (e.g., file access requests)

LiteStream Viewer allows users to view files without downloading them permanently to their device.

LiteStream Viewer may display or provide access to content hosted on third-party platforms. These external services operate under their own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services. Please review their policies before interacting with them.

We take reasonable steps to protect data during transmission, including the use of secure connections where possible. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including requesting access or deletion of any data we may hold.

6. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the application. The "Effective Date" indicates the latest revision.
